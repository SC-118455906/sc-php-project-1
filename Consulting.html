Consulting Services

Accenture

Accenture is a global management consulting and professional services firm that provides strategy and consulting services.

They have been incorporated in Dublin since 2009 and have a headquarters there. They have 3 locations in Dublin including The Docks. They also have locations in America and all over Europe

Accenture has five businesses: Accenture Strategy, Accenture Consulting, Accenture Digital, Accenture Technology and Accenture Operations where they develop services and solutions for their clients.

Egan Safety Solutions

Egan Safety Solutions was founded by Eamon Egan in 2001. Eamon is an Electrical Engineer and Health and Safety professional who, after working as a Health and Safety Inspector decided to start his own company

Egan Safety Solutions offer a range of services including (but not limited too):General Consultancy, Project Management, Engineering and Risk Management

They started in Cork and now have offices in Cork, Dublin and Kerry

Deloitte

Deloitte Ireland provide audit,tax, consulting and corporate finance services to public and private clients.

Delotte uses a business and technological view to solve complex issues and deliver strategy and implementation help

They are situated in Cork, Dublin, Limerick and Galway and employ nearly 3000 people in Ireland alone

PWC

PWC offer a broad range of services including: Audit, Tax and Consulting. Their consulting services have the goal of helping businesses grow faster and become smarter

They work to help their clients to innovate, grow, reduce costs and better utilise talent

PwC employs over 3000 people in seven locations in Ireland - Dublin, Cork, Galway, Kilkenny, Limerick, Waterford and Wexford.
